Why Kerala is the best place for a beach wedding in India

Kerala, a southern state in India, is a completely picturesque destination. It is adorned with lush greenery and coconut trees, making it an idyllic event destination for your dream wedding events. The entire state is a lush canvas of green landscapes, creating an enchanting atmosphere in all areas of the state. The Kerala state government is actively promoting destination weddings, with Kerala Public Works Department and Tourism Department Minister Muhammed Riyas recently encouraging tourism in this direction.
Benefiting from a tropical climate throughout the year, Kerala is an ideal choice for wedding events, even during the monsoon season. With a vast coastal area, Kerala offers an abundance of venues for your special day. The state’s higher education level and literacy rates ensure local expertise, streamlining the event planning process.
Geographically diverse, Kerala transitions seamlessly from captivating coastlines to elevated hill stations like Munnar and Wayanad. The renowned backwaters, a unique Kerala specialty, provide a captivating backdrop for various wedding ceremonies.
Immerse yourself in Kerala’s rich cultural traditions with local art forms like Kathakali, Mohiniyattam, oppana dance, and Kerala nadanam. Kathakali and Kalaripayattu add a distinctive charm to your wedding celebrations. Well-connected airports within a 30-kilometer radius of coastal areas ensure convenient accessibility for you and your guests.
Leverage Kerala’s holistic offerings, including Ayurvedic treatments and spa facilities, for pre- or post-wedding relaxation.
